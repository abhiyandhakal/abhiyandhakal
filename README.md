# Abhiyan Dhakal

I am a systems researcher and research engineer based in Nepal. I work on resource-aware systems, reproducible computational infrastructure, and tools that make experimental work easier to inspect and repeat.

My applied research also examines information retrieval for low-resource legal systems, with papers presented at ICAIL 2025 and ICAIL 2026.

## Current research

I am studying whether Linux Pressure Stall Information can support memory-policy decisions that improve defined tail-behavior outcomes under controlled workloads without introducing unacceptable overhead. The work is ongoing; I am currently building reproducible baselines, repeated-run infrastructure, and explicit claim boundaries.

## Selected software

- [**Strata**](https://github.com/abhiyandhakal/strata) - a terminal-first notebook with a human-editable format, Jupyter interoperability, kernels, and checkpointed state.
- [**admux**](https://github.com/abhiyandhakal/admux) - reproducible terminal workspaces with declarative layouts, persistent sessions, and best-effort restoration.
- [**autolit**](https://github.com/abhiyandhakal/autolit) - a fork maintained as the public code companion for the semantic-similarity literature-review work.
- [**SSM**](https://github.com/abhiyandhakal/ssm) - earlier tmux session-management work that preceded the broader workspace model explored in admux.
